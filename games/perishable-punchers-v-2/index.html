Perishable Punchers V.2

You may think that this is just some copy of my original game, Perishable Punchers, but NO. This is a version I made with LibGDX, which means it is available for Android as well as PC. I spend a whole week on this version, since I was just porting my old game. That doesn't mean I didn't add any features to it though.

Some of the new features incude: a secret new character, shield, numbered health bars, and ANIMATIONS. These animations will blow your mind, they're so...okay. Available for Android!
