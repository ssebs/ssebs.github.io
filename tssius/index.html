Nearly Half a Million Square Feet to Meet Your Needs

 

Technology Solutions & Services, Inc. (TSSI) is a returns management company specializing in high-volume re-manufacturing of in warranty and out of warranty consumer electronics products. We operate from three U.S. locations and two Maquiladora factories just across the border in Mexico totaling nearly 500,000 square feet. Each factory is packed with the latest testing technologies designed to move product through the refurbishment process efficiently.

Both contain focused cosmetic recovery processes to bring less-than-desirable products back to a like new sellable condition. Our goal is to provide clients with a cost effective solution that allows them to return their products back to market quickly.

Services

Technology Solutions & Services, Inc. (TSSI) is a returns management company specializing in high-volume remanufacturing of in-warranty and out-of-warranty consumer electronics products. TSSI operates two low-cost Maquiladora factories just across the border in Mexico totaling nearly 500,000 square feet, and three U.S. offices to serve clients. Each factory possesses cutting edge technologies for both technical and cosmetic recovery, making TSSI a front runner for IT, Mobile, Tablet and Wireless Networking service. In addition to test and repair, we emphasize focused cosmetic recovery processes that bring less-than-desirable products back to a like new sellable condition, reducing scrap rates while increasing product yields and recovery. Our goal is to provide clients with cost effective solutions that facilitate maximum financial recovery.

TSSI not only provides full service asset recovery solutions, but also presents manufacturers with a comprehensive analysis of their managed products. TSSI’s global partner program consists of a network of international buyers specializing in varying market segments. This network ensures you of a maximum return on liquidation items in various stages.
