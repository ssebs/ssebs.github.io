I have made a forge mod for Minecraft 1.7.10. The mod includes: new tools, weapons, biome, dimension, and a new mob.

 

Link to my Mod (ADD LINK)

 

    Some instructions for playing with the mod:

 

  • Mine the OPOre from the ground, its spawn rate is similar to that of iron.
  • Cook the OPOre and get the ingots from it.
  • Make the OPIngots into tools.
  • Make a portal in the shape of a nether portal using the OPBlocks.
  • Light this portal using an OPIgniter (Made from a cooked OPIngot).
  • Once your are in the OPDimension, you will be able to find a lot of diamonds.
